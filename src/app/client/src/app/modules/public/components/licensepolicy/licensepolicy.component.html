The EkStep Foundation (the “ Foundation ”) believes that open Assets i.e. Asset that is available free and for public use provide immense opportunities for improving access to scarce resources to create solutions and programs for various societal challenges. Open Assets can be reused within the terms of the license and modified within the terms of the license permitted. Source material is given appropriate credit and attribution, in accordance with the terms of its license. Accordingly, the Foundation follows the principles of open Assets shared as public goods on its SPace Portal.

Defined terms that are not defined here shall have the meaning ascribed to them in the Terms of Use.

What are the license options?

An Asset uploaded or linked on the SPace Portal can either be a piece of content or a software code as more clearly defined in the Terms of Use. The licenses applicable for different types of Assets are as follows:

1. Content based Asset

All content Assets on the SPace Portal are licensed under the Creative Commons license framework - CC-BY (4.0) , CC-BY-SA (4.0) , CC-BY-NC (4.0) and CC-BY-ND (4.0) . Creative commons licenses allow the creators to retain copyright while allowing others to copy, distribute and make some use of the work under conditions specified for each license type, including attribution. Anyone can use a content Asset available on the SPace Portal for their work so long as they are adhering to the requirements of the Creative Commons license as chosen by the Creator.

2. Software based Asset

The SPace Portal requires that all software Assets are licensed under either of the license options below:

  • ● MIT License

    • A software Asset can be licensed under MIT License which is a permissive-free software license originating at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Link to the license is available here - MIT license

  • ● Apache License

    • A software Asset can also be licensed under the Apache License which is a permissive-free software license written by the Apache Software Foundation. Link to the license is available here - Apache license
